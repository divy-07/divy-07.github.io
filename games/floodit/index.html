Description / How to play

Objective and Functionality

The basic objective is to flood the whole grid with the same color. The flood starts in the top-left corner and expands.
When you click a tile, the top left tile changes its color to the same as clicked tile. All the tiles adjacent to it with the same color also change to this new color.
Each of these color switches is counted as a move. The objective is to flood the entire grid in certain amount of moves. (This target is displayed on the right)

Modes

The game is customizable in two ways: size of grid and number of colors.
These are changable from the right side dropdown for each. (Must click play again after changing them to take effect)
The target score will also change when you change one of these.
